Send a message to appsbeheerder@lichtgolf.nl. Please mention which device you use, which version of the app (shown at the bottom of the settings) and what exactly went wrong.

Frequently asked questions

The app does not scan my code

Check whether it is a format that can be read — see the list on the home page. Make sure there is enough light, hold the device steady, and fill the viewfinder with the code without getting too close.

I created a code but the app will not read it back

Seven formats can be created but not read: EAN-2, EAN-5, GS1-128, Telepen and RM4SCC, plus ISBN and ITF-14 — those last two are read, but the app calls them EAN-13 and ITF, because technically that is what they are. The app warns you about this when you pick the format.

Why does the app add a digit to my EAN?

That is the check digit. An EAN-13 consists of twelve digits plus a thirteenth calculated from the other twelve. If you type it yourself, the app verifies it instead of adding one.

Where is my history stored?

Encrypted on your own device, in the app's private folder. Nothing goes to a server. You can delete entries individually or all at once.

Can I get my history back after "delete all"?

No. That action also replaces the key, precisely so that nothing can be recovered. That is why it asks for confirmation first.
